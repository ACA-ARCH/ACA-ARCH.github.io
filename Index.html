About Us

We are an Architectural Blog founded in 2016. AislingCharlotteAmy are the founders of ACA. We are three architecture students living in Belfast. We are passionate about Architectural Theory and Discussion.

QUB Students win European Award

MArch students Tallulah Brown and Thomas Smith have recently been awarded the European Best Use of Concrete in a Student Project. At a glamorous event in Geneva both students were awarded with a trophy and scholarships.

QUB Students Battle It Out in RSUA Pub Quiz

Last Thursday was the annual RSUA Pub Quiz was held in The Black Box. Despite valiant efforts the two QUB student teams lost out to their UU counterparts.

Zaha Hadid Awarded with RIBA Gold Medal

After years of innovative new projects, changing the architecture world, Zaha Hadid has been awarded the RIBA Gold Award for her outstanding contribution to British design. At the ceremony in Portland Place, Hadid said she was "humbled and honoured".
